1) Write a program which will read from the standard input two
integers and calculate:
● The sum, difference, product, quotient of the division of the two numbers.

● The real quotient of the two numbers, the product, the difference, the remainder and the remainder of the sum of the two numbers.

● The square of the first number and the square root of the second.

2) Write a program that will read the length, in meters, of the edge of a cube and calculate the area and volume of the cube.
Also calculate the area and volume of a sphere with a radius of the same length as the edge of the sphere of the previous cube.
